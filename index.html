    About

    Cong Liu is currently a M.S. student advised by Yangang Wang at Southeast University.

    His research focus is on learning-based 3D reconstruction with physical constraints.

    He serves as the paper reviewer of TVCG / TMM / CVPR / ICCV and etc.
