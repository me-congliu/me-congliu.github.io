About

Research:  My research focuses on leveraging AI to advance scientific and medical research, specializing in the following aspects: fine-grained multimodal interaction, knowledge transfer of large models, sequence image generation, and 3D cardiac motion reconstruction.

Previously:  I am pursuing my M.S. degree at Vision and Cognition Lab (VCL), Southeast University, supervised by Professor Yangang Wang. Prior to this, I obtained my B.E. degree (Top 1% graduates) in Automation, Hohai University in 2022, supervised by Professor Haoqian Huang .
